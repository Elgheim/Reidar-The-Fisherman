Brønnøya

A smal walk from Nesøya, you will find beauftifull landscape and amazing fishing.
There are no cars at Brønnøya, and even though there are a lot of cabins, theres a peacefullness out here. At the south-east end, you will have a mightfull view over the Oslofjord. If I want mackerel, this is were I go. Remember to bring a bucket, bacause if you get one, a lot more is coming!
I usually use cut herring, but if you don't have that at hand, a spoon lure also does the trick.
