Filipstad

Urban fishing in all its glory. It might smell a lot like coffee, but inn the late summer, even the makrell comes!
Sitting at the pir, concrete under your feet and a fishingrod in your hand. There's a contrast there, I find apealling. You don't need fancy gear or even hicking boots. If you have patience, this is the best place for cod.
Here a deep diving crankbait, is the best way to go. Let it lay 0,5 meter over the bottom and gently nap at it.
