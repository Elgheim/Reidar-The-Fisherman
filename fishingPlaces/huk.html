Huk

Huk is maybe most known for their beach, but there is also trout to be found!
A hot summer day in July might not be the best time of the year to visit this place, but as soon as the temperature is colder, the people get fewer and the fishes get more. Pollock, sea trout, cod and mackerel are some of the favourites you can catch here.
Here I usually use a universal fishing lure, because the catch rate is very good, and you can get all kinds of suprises.
